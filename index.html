Welcome

TY Compass was created by a parent to make it easier for a TY student to focus on where to look for work experience.

Lining up two weeks is hard — and with more people working from home, some companies find it harder to support a TY student. Spots are limited.

So instead of applying everywhere and hoping, TY Compass looks at what you enjoy and how you like to work, then points you toward a focused group of places worth approaching — ones with a real chance of saying yes. You get a better shot, and you're not taking a spot someone else was set on.

No right or wrong answers, about 5 minutes. 🧭

TY Compass uses AI to spot patterns in your answers — things you enjoy, how you like to work, what matters to you — and suggests the kinds of places that might be worth approaching for work experience. It is not a career test and it does not predict your future. Use it as a starting point, and chat to your guidance counsellor or a trusted adult too.

What do you enjoy?

Think about what you genuinely like — not what you're told you're good at, or what gets the best marks. What do you actually enjoy spending time on?

How do you like to work?

There's no better or worse here — everyone's different. Just pick what feels most like you.

What matters to you?

A few last questions about what you care about and what you enjoy outside school.
